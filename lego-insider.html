What is a LEGO® Account?

A LEGO® Account gives you access to all digital LEGO® experiences. If you're aged 18 or older, you can also use your account when shopping on LEGO.com.

Please consider: You must be 18 years or older to shop online. Using your child's information when signing up will prevent you from completing a purchase.
